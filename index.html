It's Not Who I Am Underneath But What I Do That Defines Me.

Overcoming Fear and Finding Purpose: In "Batman Begins," Bruce Wayne learns to conquer his fear and channel it into a force for good. He understands that fear can be a tool to protect others and bring justice to a corrupt world. This teaches us the importance of facing our fears and using our strengths for a higher purpose.
